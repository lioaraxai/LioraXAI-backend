About LioraXAI

We're on a mission to make knowledge accessible and actionable for every organization.

Our Story

Founded in 2021, LioraXAI was born from a simple observation: companies struggle to make their valuable knowledge accessible to the people who need it, when they need it.

Our founders, having worked at large enterprises and fast-growing startups, experienced firsthand the frustration of information silos and knowledge gaps. They saw how much time employees spent searching for answers, and how much productivity was lost in the process.

With backgrounds in artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and enterprise software, they set out to build a solution that would transform how companies manage and access their collective knowledge.

Today, LioraXAI helps organizations of all sizes unlock the power of their knowledge base through our AI-powered platform. Our mission is to make information instantly accessible, reducing friction and enabling teams to focus on what matters most.
