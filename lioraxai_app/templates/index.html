What's our refund policy for enterprise customers?

Enterprise customers have a 30-day money-back guarantee on all subscription tiers. For annual plans, refunds are prorated after the first 30 days according to the following schedule:

  • Within 90 days: 75% refund of remaining months
  • 91-180 days: 50% refund of remaining months
  • After 180 days: 25% refund of remaining months

Source: Enterprise Terms & Conditions (Updated March 2023)

Do we offer extensions on the 30-day guarantee for clients in implementation?

Yes, for enterprise customers in active implementation, the 30-day guarantee period begins after implementation is complete, not from the contract signing date. Your account manager can approve extensions up to 60 additional days for complex deployments.

Source: Sales Handbook, Section 4.3: Enterprise Accommodations
